Home workout

Workout every 2 days, alternating between workouts A and B. Complete the whole workout 2 to 3 times, with no rest between sets and exercises. Each exercise should run for about a minute.

Credits to Jeff Cavaliere, athleanx.com. As seen on Youtube.

Workout A

SQUAT / ANTERIOR

  • Alt. Single Leg Box Squats
  • 1 1/2 Bottomed Out Squats
  • Jump Squats

UPPER PUSH

  • Handstand Pushups OR Power Pushaways
  • Rotational Pushups
  • Cobra Pushups (long head contracted)

HINGE / POSTERIOR

  • Alt. Single Leg Heel Touch Squats
  • Alt. Sprinter Lunges
  • Plyo Sprinter Lunges

UPPER PULL

  • Pullups
  • Human Pullovers OR BW Sliding Pulldowns
  • Inverted Chin Curls

ABS

  • Reverse Corkscrews
  • Black Widow Knee Slides
  • Levitation Crunches

UPPER PUSH

Angels and Devils

Workout B

HINGE / POSTERIOR

  • Slick Floor Bridge Curl (Single Leg) OR Slick Floor Bridge Curl (Dual Leg)
  • Long Leg March
  • High Hip Bucks

UPPER PUSH

  • Variable Wall Pushups OR Knee Decline Pushups to Knee Flat Pushups
  • Alt. BW Side Lateral Raises
  • Tricep BW Extensions

LUNGE / ANTERIOR

  • Alt. Crossover Step Ups
  • Alt. Reverse Lunges
  • Split Squat Jumps

UPPER PULL

  • Chin Ups OR Seated Chin Ups
  • Inverted Rows
  • Back Widows

ABS x 2 ROUNDS

  • Ab Halos
  • V-Up Tucks
  • Sit-Up Elbow Thrusts

CORRECTIVE x 2 ROUNDS

Reverse Hypers
